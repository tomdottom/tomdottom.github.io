Software Engineering Podcasts

My Top Podcasts related to Software-Engineering, Python, & Javascript

I spend an inordinate amount of time listening to podcasts of which software engineering themed podcasts take up a lot of space on my iPod.

I find them immessly useful in introducing me to new concepts, packages, books, resources, etc...

I listen to all of the below on and off unless otherwise indicated and hope you might find something here for you too:

General Software Engineering

  • Software Engineering Radio

    Published by the IEEE Computer Society its flagship podcast Software Engineering Radio covers a wide range topics from the foremost engineers in the world. I pick and choose episodes that seem relevant to me, but recent gems include:

    • SE-Radio Episode 281: James Whittaker on Career Strategy
    • SE-Radio Episode 280: Gerald Weinberg on Bugs Errors and Software Quality
    • SE-Radio Episode 278: Peter Hilton on Naming
    • SE-Radio Episode 273: Steve McConnell on Software Estimation

Python

Javascript

  • Javascript Jabber

    Just one of many offerings from Dev Chat Tv. Until my most recent job I had listened to every episode. The podcast can almost be considered a documentry of the explosive development of JS over the past five or so years.

    In particular I can directly attribute a direct improvement in my JS skill to Episode 070: Book Club - Javascript Alonge with Reginald Braitwaite which lead me to read the excellent Javascript Allonge and interesting Functional Javascript.
